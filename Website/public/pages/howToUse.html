How To Use
Even if our goal is to make astronomical observations more clear, sometimes it can be very difficult for individuals. Please walk through the tutorial and don’t forget to contact us.
In the homepage of Bohredom you will see three instrument groups that has different images with variety of wavelengths. You can click on them and see 360° combined skymaps. These skymaps show 100% of sky and can be downloaded with a single button click.

And here is the map! You can look around by dragging your mouse and zoom by scrolling. You will see three buttons: at the left hamburger menu and info, at the right filter menu respectively. These allow you to see whole map without any distraction unless you wanted to. Let’s see how these buttons work.

By clicking select filter button you will see a menu like that. You can move around the instrument groups with the dropdown menu on top. Other six buttons will show the whole sky in different wavelengths. Note that, some instrument groups cannot get some data of wavelengths.

If you desire to turn back to home page whilst you are travelling at 360° combined sky map, you can use the hamburger menu. In the menu, it is possible to visit about, contact and how to use sections as well.

By clicking information button (or more info button in select filter menu) you will be navigated to this page. You can get information about the instrument group and wavelengths. Clicking “Go Back” will get you back the last page you visited.
